Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

ZeroHost ("we", "us", "our") is a community project providing free hosting services. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our free hosting website at dashboard.zero-host.org and related services.

2. Data We Collect on Our Free Hosting Platform

We collect minimal data. Here is what may be collected:

  • Account Information: When you register on our Pterodactyl panel, we collect your email address and username. This data is necessary to provide the service.
  • Server Data: Files and configurations you upload to your hosted servers are stored on our infrastructure.
  • Logs: Server access logs and error logs may be retained for security and debugging purposes.

3. Cookies and Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following external resources that may set cookies or collect data:

  • Google Fonts: When you accept, fonts are loaded from Google's servers. Google may collect your IP address and browser information. You can decline this loading via the cookie consent banner.
  • CDN Resources: Static assets (icons, images) are loaded from third-party CDNs (jsdelivr, SimpleIcons). These may log your IP address temporarily.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking tools.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data solely for:

  • Providing and maintaining the hosting service
  • Communicating with you about your account
  • Ensuring security and preventing abuse
  • Improving our service

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data and server files will be removed within 30 days.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of ZeroHost and its users
  • With service providers who assist in operating our infrastructure (under strict data processing agreements)

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your data.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent at any time (e.g., via Cookie Settings).

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Data Security on Free Hosting

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your free hosting data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), regular backups, and access controls on our infrastructure.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us via our status page or through the Pterodactyl panel.
